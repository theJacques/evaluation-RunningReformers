What is Friday Night Youth all about?

Every Friday we get together to connect, learn and have some fun!

Games

We start of with some icebreakers to get to know each other and just to have some fun in a safe environment.

Praise and worship

We spend some time to praise and worship.

Teaching of the word

Every Friday night we discuss a different topic. Herman, our youth Pastor, will talk about a certain topic and afterwards you, the youth, will discuss it furhter in small groups.

When and Where

Date

Every Friday night except for school holidays.
