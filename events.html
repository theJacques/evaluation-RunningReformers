What is Friday Night Youth all about?

Every Friday we get together to connect, learn and have some fun!

Games

We start of with some icebreakers to get to know each other and just to have some fun in a safe environment. Warning, you might get dirty.

Praise and worship

We spend some time to praise and worship. We have our own band to lead us in worship. This will prepare us for the scripture that will follow.

Teaching of the word

Every Friday night we discuss a different topic. Herman, our youth Pastor, will talk about a certain topic and afterwards you will discuss it further in small groups.

When and Where

Date

Every Friday night except for school holidays.
Keep an eye on Instagram to get notified of any changements.
